Drills

Train the right way

Pick a predefined drill or build your own under Settings → Configure Drills. Choose from four drill types:

Trackman Combine & Shots Gained +

The classic Combine test. For every club except the driver you get a target and try to land your carry as close to it as possible — the closer you are, the higher the score. For the driver, hit as long (total distance) and as straight as possible.

On top of the Trackman score, the app shows the shots you gained (positive) or lost (negative) compared to the average PGA Tour player.

Spin +

Produce stopping power. Each spin drill defines a landing corridor; land inside it and your score scales linearly with the total spin you generate per meter/yard. Hit the target spin and you score 100 — double it and you score 200.

Optionally penalize long coasting to reward true "stopping power", with configurable thresholds for negative scoring.

Target Circle +

Great for pitch training. Two circles define a score of 100 (e.g. 2 yd) and 0 (e.g. 11 yd) around target distances. Between them the score is linear; inside the inner circle it keeps climbing above 100.

As Few Strokes As Possible +

A progression variant of the target-circle drill. Land in the circle and advance to the next distance; miss and you choose what happens — retry, drop back, or restart. The goal: finish all distances in as few shots as possible.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does TrajectoryX cost? +

The app is 100% free, including a high-quality ball flight model designed in-house. Two extras are optional: the Foresight Sports ball flight model and unlimited swing video. You can try them every day for free — 5 shots with the Foresight model and 3 swing videos per day — and unlock them fully with an optional subscription.

It's for new subscribers and anyone whose Pro subscription has lapsed — if you already have an active Pro subscription, the code can't be applied. Redeem it in the App Store, or in the app under Settings → Subscriptions → Redeem Promo Code. It renews at the standard price afterwards, and you can cancel anytime.

Which launch monitors are supported? +

TrajectoryX works with the Foresight Sports GCQuad, QuadMAX and GC3, as well as the Garmin Approach R10 via the E6 Connect protocol. The app connects directly to your launch monitor over Wi-Fi — for the GC3 either through its own hotspot or over your local network.

Do I need an internet connection? +

No — your practice works fully offline and all your data is stored on your device. An internet connection is only needed for online competitions and leaderboards, and for the optional AI Coach.

Is my data private? +

Yes. Everything is saved on-device by default and nothing is sent to any server. The only exception is the optional AI Coach: when you enable it and add your own API key, your shot data is sent to the AI provider you chose. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How does the AI Coach work? +

The AI Coach analyzes your session and gives you an overall grade, your strengths, what to improve and a targeted drill. You bring your own API key for Claude, ChatGPT or Gemini and choose the provider and model — your key stays on your device.

What devices does TrajectoryX run on? +

TrajectoryX is a native app for iPhone and iPad. The layout adapts to both, with extra data shown on larger screens and in landscape.

How does swing video work? +

Hit your shot and TrajectoryX records your swing on its own, then plays it back in slow motion — it detects the key swing points (Start, Top, Impact, End) so you can jump straight to them and step through frame by frame. No launch monitor required, and it pairs perfectly with one.

On-video reference lines (spine, head, ball line, swing plane) and multi-angle recording with several devices are planned for a later update.

Does swing video work indoors and outdoors? +

Outdoors it works great — with or without a launch monitor. Indoors, we recommend using a launch monitor: it tells the app exactly when you strike the ball, so every swing is captured cleanly. Recording indoors without a launch monitor is still experimental today and keeps getting better with every update.

Can I move my sessions to another device? +

Yes — you can export a whole practice session, including every shot and its swing videos, as a single file and import it on another iPhone or iPad. It's ideal for backing up your best sessions, switching devices or sharing a session with your coach — no cloud account needed.
