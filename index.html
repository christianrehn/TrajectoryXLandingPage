Practice Range Shots, Combine Tests, Chip and Pitch Drills with your Foresight Sports GCQuad

Practice in Range Mode .

Set up you environment (temperature, humidity, elevation, distance/speed/temperature units, ...) and start practicing with different clubs. See ball, club and trajectory data for the current shot or all shots in current session. Visualize ball trajectory in 2D (carry and offline).

Improve Your Wedge Play and Approach Game .

Choose from predefined Drills or tailor them to your needs. Do TM Combine Tests (without driver) or increase backspin. Pitch to random or fixed distances and see how many shots you gained against the tour average.

Connect GCQuad's Wi-Fi

Connect your iPhone/iPad to your launch monitor's Wi-Fi in the iOS Settings app.

Connect TrajectoryX App

Initiate a launch monitor connection from the TrajectoryX App by clicking the connect botton.

Start your training

As soon as the connection status is green the ball and club data provided by your launch monitor is processed and trajectory data calculated.

Analyze ball and club data

Check and compare all your shots in a range or drill session. For drills different scores, averages and consistency values are calculated.
